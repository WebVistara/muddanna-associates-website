Founder & Advocate

Mohan Kumar M

Advocate | Founder

Mohan Kumar M is a practicing advocate with over 9 years of legal experience, specializing in constitutional and civil law matters. He holds a Master's Degree in Economics from Janabharathi Campus, Bengaluru, and completed his LL.B from KLE Society, Bengaluru.

Over the years, he has successfully handled cases involving property disputes, money recovery suits, contract-related matters, family disputes, cheque bounce cases, and other civil proceedings. He has actively represented clients before the High Court of Karnataka and various District Courts, building a reputation for dedicated advocacy and strategic legal representation.

Who we are

Firm Introduction

Muddanna Associates is a Bengaluru-based legal practice committed to delivering reliable and strategic legal services across a wide range of civil and constitutional matters,property disputes,money recovery cases.Founded by Advocate Mohan Kumar, the firm combines strong academic grounding with practical courtroom experience to provide effective representation and trusted legal counsel.

With a client-focused approach, the firm handles matters related to constitutional law, property disputes, financial and contractual disputes, family matters, cheque bounce cases, and various civil proceedings. Known for professionalism, integrity, and attention to detail, the firm aims to protect client interests while navigating the complexities of the legal system.

Headquartered in Bengaluru, Muddanna Associates represents clients before the High Court of Karnataka and various District Courts, offering dependable advocacy and legal guidance.
